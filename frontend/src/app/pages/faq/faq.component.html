How To

What is my "Family Code"?

Your Family Code is a unique identifier to help you locate your family on the site. It will allow both members and nonmembers to join your family. All you have to do is manually share the code, or, you can press the "Share Code" button to send them an email!

How do I find my "Family Code"?

Your Family Code can be found in your Manage Family tab.

What if I'm sick of my family and want to leave it?

We get it. All you have to do is click the "Leave Family" button in your Manage Family tab. You can always rejoin a family by entering their Family Code!
